Causes of Air Pollution

1. Industrial Emissions

Factories and industries release pollutants into the air, contributing to air pollution.

2. Vehicle Emissions

The burning of fossil fuels in vehicles releases harmful gases and particulate matter.

Effects of Air Pollution

1. Respiratory Issues

Long-term exposure to air pollution can lead to respiratory diseases and conditions.

2. Environmental Damage

Air pollution harms the environment, affecting ecosystems, wildlife, and natural resources.
